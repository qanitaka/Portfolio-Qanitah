About Me

Hi! I’m Qanitah Khaula A., a passionate Informatics Engineering student with a strong background in multimedia and digital creation. Before entering university, I studied Multimedia during vocational high school, where I explored various aspects of visual communication, design, and technology.

During that time, I actively participated in several short movie projects with my classmates and seniors, working on documentation, video production, and storytelling. I also learned photography and videography techniques — from framing, lighting, and angle composition to post-editing — which strengthened my sense of visual aesthetics.

I’m skilled in various creative tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and After Effects. I’ve also created both 2D and 3D animations and designed visual assets for digital projects. Besides the creative side, I started learning HTML, CSS, and PHP in high school — which became my first step into web development.

I continue to expand my skills by learning Figma for UI/UX design and focusing on how to build engaging, user-friendly, and visually appealing web interfaces. My goal is to combine art and technology to craft meaningful digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional.
